About Us

Situated in the heart of Providence, we're a family dedicated to the mission of bring authentic Sichuanese cuisine to Rhode Island. From our humble origins operating out of our garage, Chengdu Taste has been voted Providence’s best Chinese 3 times. We’re blending tradition with innovation, bringing a simultaneously classic and modern twist to Chinese cuisine.

If you're looking for Sichuan we're the best in town. Period.

Menu

Located in the heartland of Central China, Sichuan is renowed for its unique combination of spicy yet numbing cuisine. There's a saying in Chengdu: if it's not spicy, it's not good. Enjoy the Sichuan classics of Lazi Ji Ding, Mala Xiang Guo, Dan Dan Mian, and much more!
